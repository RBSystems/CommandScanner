Perform network scan
ACTIVESCAN [TSID|ALL] [VERBOSE] [SCAN CHANNELS MASK] [SCAN DURATION] [SCAN COUNT] 
	TSID               - TSID of the gateway/remote device.
	ALL                - every device in the network will perform energy scans with provided parameters
	VERBOSE            - display the results ON/OFF at the end of scan or RESULTSONLY
	SCAN CHANNELS      - 0 - all channels, 11-26 individual channels (defaults to all channels)
	SCAN DURATION      - how long to scan on each channel - 0=31ms,1=46ms,2=77ms,3=138ms,4=261ms,5=507ms (default)
	SCAN COUNT         - number of scans to be performed on each channel - 1-default

	REFORM - release existing device(s)

		DeviceClass - classes of devices that are eligible for NAF events. range (0..0xFFFF in HEX) 
		FloorIndex -  floor effected by NAF events. range (0..255 in decimal) 
		RankingProfile - performance ranking profile. range (0..7 in decimal) 
	STOP - stop auto formation 
	No parameter - displays current setting
